The buildable half of M7 infra: the publishing pipeline that turns a `brokers.json`
into a signed, hosted pair. Extension side is already done and inert until keys are
pinned (see [[P-010-m7-dataset-go-live]]). Source: `plan/dataset-delivery-runbook.md`
(steps 1, 4, 5, 6) and `plan/dataset-delivery.md` §8. Decisions locked: Posture B,
host `data.expurge.com`, WebCrypto.

The publishing pipeline lives in the standalone repo **`Occams-Garage/expurge-data`** (pushed
2026-07-21; separate blast radius). NOTE: two implementations converged here. A from-scratch
scaffold was built in the extension tree, but a more mature parallel repo already existed; per the
2026-07-21 decision the parallel repo is canonical and the scaffold's two unique pieces (author-
gated trust-guard + a `node --test` suite) were merged into it. The canonical design differs from
the scaffold: a **source/generated split** (`data/brokers.data.json` -> `scripts/build-dataset.mjs`
-> signed `site/brokers.json`), and **no CI-side anti-rollback** (deliberate: the extension enforces
it; documented in the repo README). Enabling Pages + DNS is the human handoff in
[[P-010-m7-dataset-go-live]].
