1.5 — Voice & tone

Calm, plain, and honest

We never alarm, never nag, and never inflate the threat to push action. We use plain language, take the user’s side, and point them to better options when they exist — even when that means they need us less.

plain-language never alarming never naggy on the user’s side points to better options
